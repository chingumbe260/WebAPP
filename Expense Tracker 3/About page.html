What the App Does

Track Your Spending

Expense Tracker allows you to record every expense you make. Whether it's groceries, transport, or bills, you can easily log it and monitor your spending habits in real time.

Create Monthly Budgets

Plan your finances by setting monthly budgets for different categories. This helps you stay within your limits and avoid unnecessary overspending.

Envelope Budgeting System

Our app uses a simple envelope budgeting method where you allocate money to categories. Once a category is used up, you know it's time to stop spending.

Simple and Easy to Use

The app is designed for everyone — no accounting knowledge required. Just sign up, add your income, and start tracking your expenses.

Stay in Control

By clearly seeing where your money goes, you can make smarter financial decisions and build better saving habits over time.

Private & Secure

Your financial data belongs to you. We keep your information safe and do not share it with anyone.
